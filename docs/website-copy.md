## Why people choose it

### You hold the keys
Your recovery phrase and coins live on your device and nowhere else. We can't see your balance, freeze your funds, or move them. That's the whole point of a self-custody wallet — you're in control, always.

### Ready in a minute
Create a wallet, jot down your recovery phrase, and you're done. Sending and receiving feel as easy as any payment app — because they should.

### Private by default
No sign-up. No email. No analytics following you around. The wallet talks to the network to do its job and keeps your business your business.

### Beautiful on both phones
Built natively for iPhone and Android, so it feels right at home on either one — quick, clean, and easy on the eyes in light or dark mode.

## Features

### Send and receive
Scan a QR code or paste an address, pick how fast you want it to go, review everything on one clear screen, and send. Receiving is just as simple — show your QR code or share your address in a tap.

### Multiple wallets, one app
Keep separate wallets for separate things — everyday spending, long-term savings, a wallet you share with family. Each one stands on its own, with its own recovery phrase. Switch between them instantly.

### Your history, clearly
Every transaction in a tidy timeline: what came in, what went out, when it confirmed, and where it went. Tap any one for the full details or to view it on a block explorer.

### Fees you understand
Choose slow, normal, or fast before you send, and always see the total before you confirm. No surprises, no guesswork.

### Live prices
See what your balance is worth in your local currency, updated as you go.

## Security you can feel

### Lock it down
Protect the app with Face ID, fingerprint, or your passcode — on open, and again right before you send. Step away and it quietly locks itself.

### Your recovery phrase, done right
When you back up, the wallet walks you through saving your phrase and then checks that you got it down correctly. It even warns you about the common scams that try to trick people out of it.

### Nothing to leak
Your private keys are only ever unlocked for the single moment it takes to sign a payment, then locked away again. They're never stored in the open and never leave your phone.

### Built on proven foundations
The wallet's core is powered by the same battle-tested open-source engine trusted across the Bitcoin world — not something we hand-rolled.

## FAQ

**Do I need an account?**
No. There's no sign-up, no email, nothing to create. Your wallet is generated right on your device.

**Is it really free?**
Yes. The app is free and open source. The only cost of sending is the network fee, which goes to the network — not to us.

**What happens if I lose my phone?**
As long as you've saved your recovery phrase, you can restore your wallet on a new device and pick up right where you left off. That phrase is the master key — keep it safe and never share it.

**Can you access my coins?**
No. We never hold your keys and can't see, move, or freeze your funds. Self-custody means it's all you.

**iPhone or Android?**
Both. The wallet is built natively for each, so you get a first-class experience either way.
